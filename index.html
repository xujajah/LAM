About Massage

One of the oldest healing techniques. A natural remedy against pain, it was and is an instrument of communication. Where knowledge and experience are transmitted through direct contact. This knowledge has passed from generation to generation and today the massages have specialized according to the needs, to obtain relief, relaxation or prevent injuries and even diseases.

Our team

We are a group of accredited masseurs who continually specialize in new techniques or methods and not only within the area of massage but also in areas like fitness or nutrition. Among us we share the knowledge and experience to achieve the best treatment and service for our clients and to be ready for any situation.

Before Massage

You should consider some contraindications. For example, inflammations, fever, allergies, skin diseases, burns, wounds with bleeding, period of menstruation or cases such as cancer. It is important to inform in case of pregnancy. At the time of the massage, remember that it is a direct contact treatment where you should inform the therapist if you have an uncomfortable situation.

Services

Classic massage

This massage helps you to relax your entire body from feet to neck. where your muscles will tonify, and your blood circulation will improve therefore you will feel lighter. In this procedure will not feel heavy pressure.

Reconditioning massage

This massage is recommended for people who carry an average physical activity whether they carry out routine work or practice sports without any training. Mobility problems of the body are present as well fatigue. It works focused on muscles and joints

Deep Tissue Massage

This massage is not for the whole body and is not long lasting, it focuses on the problematic parts with recurrence. It can cause pain during the procedure, since it is necessary to use a deep manipulation to achieve stretching of tissues such as ligament, insertions or facies.

Sports Massage

This massage gives more emphasis on preparing the muscles, ligaments and joints for a physical activity that requires effort. It is also indicated for people returning from a long period of rest or to prevent injuries before a competition..

Relaxing Massage

We use the bases of a classic massage for the whole body with the difference that the pressure is softer and synchronized. We use more oil and we can even add a little aroma to get faster to a point of tranquility and peace. It is recommended for people who want to disconnect the mind from the tasks of the day. You can ask your therapist for other relaxation massage techniques like Lomi Lomi or Aroma massage

Prenatal Massage

It is very important to keep the muscles and the whole body in a good balance even during the period of pregnancy. Our therapists have the knowledge and experience necessary to provide care and relaxation during this important period.
